♠ PRIVACY POLICY ♥

Home · Play · Terms of Service

Last updated July 2026

Table Seven ("we," "the site") is a free, play-money blackjack game. This policy explains, in plain language, what little information the site collects and why.

The short version: we don't sell anything, there's no real money involved, and we don't run ads or third-party trackers. The only data we keep is what's needed to remember your play-money balance between visits and to keep the game running smoothly.

What we collect

  • The name you type in. Shown at the table to other players. You can use any name — we don't verify identity.
  • A cage account: your play-money balance, a randomly generated Player # and PIN (so you can log back in if your browser clears its cookies), and how many times you've been comped.
  • Gameplay history: bets, hands played, and results, tied to your account, so the Pit Boss admin console can review activity and so you can trust the game is being played fairly.
  • A random token stored in your browser's local storage that identifies your account on future visits. It contains no personal information by itself.
  • IP address, used only in memory to rate-limit abusive traffic (for example, someone hammering the login endpoint). We don't log or store IP addresses long-term.

What we don't collect

  • No real names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses (unless you choose to type one as your display name, which we'd recommend against).
  • No payment information of any kind — there is nothing to buy and nothing to cash out for real money.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising pixels, or trackers.
  • No cookies used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

How your data is stored

Account balances and gameplay history are stored in a database we operate. Live table state (cards, bets, chat/log lines) exists only in server memory while a table is active and is not permanently retained once the table closes.

Who can see it

The site operator can view account balances, transaction history, and gameplay history through an internal admin console, protected by a private key. Other players only ever see your display name, chip count, and cards at the table — never your Player #, PIN, or account history.

Children's privacy

Table Seven is not directed at children, does not intentionally collect information from anyone under 13, and involves no real-money gambling of any kind.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the site operator through foundinvegas.com.
